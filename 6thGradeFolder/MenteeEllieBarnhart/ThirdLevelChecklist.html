In this level, you will be focusing on Recycling and giving to people. Recycling is wonderful for the environment because it can help reduce the amount of pollution in the environment. For this level's challenge, an established mayor is worried about the anual "Giving Back" ceremony that is in two days. She was supposed to show the people that she is qualified for the mayor by doing something that will help the world. She forgot and needs your help to save "Giving Back" day. She is willing to pay you 300 gold pieces for every checkbox you complete or if you complete two checkboxes and correctly answer a Trivia question. You have an chance to win 900 gold pieces if you complete everything.



Try to reuse empty packages for other uses (A cardboard box as a storage box), (250 experience points)

Research where items should go in waste bins. For example; Food in Compost, Tin foil in the Recycle bin and, Styrofoam in Landfill (200 experience points)

Donate all unneeded/already read magazines, books, newspapers to nursing homes, clinics, charity, hospitals, waiting rooms, etc. (500 experience points)
