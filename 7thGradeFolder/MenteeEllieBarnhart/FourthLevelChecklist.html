In previous levels, you have been focusing on the health of others and the health of the World. In this level, you will focus on the health of you. Being healthy is essential to how you function. If you don't sleep enough, you won't feel well the next morning. If you don't eat well, you will be unhealthy. A manager for an important business has noticed your services and decided to thank you by paying you to feel healthy. He is willing to pay you 400 gold pieces for every checkbox you complete or if you complete two checkboxes and correctly answer a Trivia question. You have an chance to win 1,200 gold pieces if you complete everything.


Walk instead of drive to a destination (300 experience points)
Plant a few flowers or other herbs to make a peace garden where you can do work (350 experience points)

If you are listening to music either with headphones or on the radio, turn it down a few notches so it stops blaring in your ears, damaging your hearing or bothering others (325 experience points)
