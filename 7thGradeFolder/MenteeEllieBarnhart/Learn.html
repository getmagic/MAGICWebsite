Start by taking the Character Quiz.

Once you have chosen a Race and a Class, a checklist of good deeds will pop up and they will help both the environment and also keep you in shape.
For each checkbox, there is a type of pollution you have to focus on.

Every time you succeed, you will get a certain number of experience points and a certain number of gold pieces, which will be totaled up in the end!

Once you have completed 2 out of the 3 checkboxes on the leveled checklist you are on, a trivia question will pop up, based on what Class you chose. If you answer the trivia question correctly, you will get a 100 experience points along with the points for the third checkbox and be transported to the next level, without having to finish the previous level.

If you answer it incorrectly, a number of experince points will be deducted and as the levels progress, the trivia questions will get harder and more experience points can be lost or gained.

If you complete all 5 levels, and finish all of the levels, you will get be congratulated on your efforts and a reminder to be aware of helping the environment. Then, if you would like, you can play again with either the same or different character!
