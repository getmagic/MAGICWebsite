Advocate

  • Raise awareness!
    Talk to friends, family and colleagues. You could even start talking to your local politicians and authorities.
  • Write a blog or social media post and share with your friends and online community.
  • Petition World Leaders:
    Urge world leaders to put human trafficking on the agenda and find a way to put an end to modern day slavery.
    Urge your representative to support critical investments to prevent human trafficking.
